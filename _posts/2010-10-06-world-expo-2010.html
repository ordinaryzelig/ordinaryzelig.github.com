Here are some things I shot at the World Expo in Shanghai.

This was the line to just get into the grounds. There was airport-like security. Believe it or not, this line only took less than an hour. The lines at the popular pavilions were not so short. The longest one was at Saudi Arabia's pavilion. When I got in line, I asked how long the wait was from where I was standing. 8+ hours. As I was leaving the line, there were still lots of people packing in behind me.

Here are some of the pavilions I filmed:

  • Canada
  • China
  • Israel
  • Italy
  • Poland
  • Russia
  • Tanzania

The expo website has an excellent page with all the pavilions. Each pavilion has their own virtual tour.
