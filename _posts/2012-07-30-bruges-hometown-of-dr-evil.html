Bruges is officially the place I'm most excited to visit so far on my trip. Just look at this place, it's so gorgeous and charming. And look at the place I'm staying. How charming is that? The owner prides herself on breakfast. 

Admitedly, the only reason I wanted to visit was because of the movie In Bruges. But then I also remembered that it's where Dr. Evil was raised to be evil. Can't wait.

$170 2 nights. Well over my $40/night budget, but seems like a worthy splurge.
