The first time I tried reading Harry Potter, I couldn't get into it. When the 1st movie was about to come out, I was in film school in college. I thought I should read the book before judging the movies because I wanted to experience the movie the same way most of the audience would too. So started reading them. Just a shade under a decade later, I finally finished them all (the books). I liked the movies a lot too, of course. I've visited the Wizarding World of Harry Potter at Universal Studios Orlando, and it was great. But this was something else. This was the real deal.

Phoenix staircase to Dumbledore's office. It actually works. It required a moving poll that extends into the ground several yards.

Dumbledore's memory cabinet. It contains hundreds of vials filled with memories. Each is hand made and hand labeled.
