The next morning Felice had a meeting in Tampa. We woke up very early and drove about an hour and half. After her hour long meeting, we went to the Tampa aquarium.

They claim to be one of the best in the country, and I would have to agree. They have penguins. They have some things that I've never seen before too.

So that was our Orlando trip. The happiest place on earth (and the only place where room service is the cheapest way to eat).
