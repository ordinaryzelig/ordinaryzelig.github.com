I watched 137 movies.

  • 4 I watched twice (Not counting those I re-watched bits of when they’re on TV).
  • 68 I had watched before.
  • 69 I watched for the 1st time (giggity).

137 movies in a little more than a year. That’s ~2.5 movies per week. Even with margin of error (those I forgot to write down, those I didn’t count because I didn’t watch the whole thing, those I didn’t pay any attention to because I was doing something else), that’s a pretty low average considering how much I love watching movies. I’m going to try to fix that for 2011.
