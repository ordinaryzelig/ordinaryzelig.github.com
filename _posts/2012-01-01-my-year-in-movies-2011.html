This year I watched 255 Movies (up from 137 from last year). That's almost 5 movies every week! It doesn't feel like I watch that many movies, but that's a winning number in my book.

99 of those movies I had never watched before compared to 156 movies that I rewatched. That's about a 2:3 ratio. My goal for 2012 is to bring that ratio closer to 1:1. But considering how much I love rewatching movies, and adding new movies to the list of movies I want to rewatch, that could be tough.

Here's my lists for 2011. And here's to a great new year of movies!
